ROBERT EDMOND ALTER

Author of adventures about alligators, crime, American heroes, thirteenth-century Saxons and Normans, science fiction and fantasy.

LIFE EVENTS:

  • Born: December 10, 1925 in San Francisco, California, USA
  • 1941: Leaves home to travel. Becomes a citrus picker, movie extra and soldier
  • June 6, 1947: Discharged from the Army (Private First class)
  • November 17, 1947: Married to Maxine Louise Outwater
  • January 23, 1949: Daughter Sand Alter born
  • Died: May 1966 (age 40)
